Welcome Message

Welcome — Watch This First

01
Week One — Get Oriented
Set up the basics. Don't try to perfect everything at once.
Read the Ignition Protocol guide — understand the why before you worry about the how. Read it here →
Start tracking your waking temperature every morning, before getting out of bed. Log it in the Temperature Tracker.
Review your nutrition targets and get familiar with the Recipe Library — find 5-6 meals you'll actually want to repeat.
Read Training & Recovery Over 40 so you understand the structure behind your programme. Read it here →
Set up your supplement stack — see Supplements: What Actually Works for the exact daily protocol.
Complete your first Weekly Check-In at the end of the week. Open the Check-In form →
02
Week Two — Build the Habit
The novelty wears off here. This is where consistency starts to matter more than precision.
Read Healthy Lifestyle Habits and pick one lever to focus on this week — sleep, morning light, or alcohol. Read it here →
Log your 1RMs for your main lifts so your training weights are dialled in. Open the 1RM Calculator →
Check in on your waking temperature trend — is it moving? Flat? Don't panic either way, just note it in your check-in.
If you're feeling overwhelmed or wired, run through the Stress Management System — start with the quick check-in at the top.
Complete your second Weekly Check-In — be honest about what's working and what isn't.
→
What to Expect
A rough timeline so you know what's normal.
WK 1-2
Adjustment
More food than you're used to. Some bloating or water retention is normal as glycogen stores fill.
WK 2-4
Early Signals
Waking temperature should start trending up. Training performance usually improves first.
WK 4-8
Momentum
Energy and sleep typically stabilise. Body composition changes start becoming visible.
WK 8-12
Clear Progress
DEXA-confirmed recomposition. This is when most clients say it "clicks."
