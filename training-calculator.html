1RM & Training
Load Calculator

Enter the weight and reps you completed on any lift to get your estimated 1-rep max and the working weights for every rep range in your programme.

How This Works

The estimated 1RM is calculated using the Epley formula: e1RM = weight × (1 + reps / 30). This is one of the most validated 1RM prediction formulas for sets of 1–10 reps.

Working weights are expressed as percentages of that estimated 1RM. These align with standard hypertrophy and strength rep ranges used in the Reboot programme. Use the 70–80% range for your main hypertrophy work (8–12 reps). The 85–90% range is used for strength blocks.

Note: estimated 1RMs become less accurate above 10 reps. For best results, use a set of 3–8 reps performed close to failure.
